Gospel Salvation Guide: Your Path to Eternal Life

The gospel is a powerful message that offers salvation to all who believe. Engaging in soul winning efforts is essential to share this message and help others understand the importance of faith in their journey towards eternal life.

The Gospel of Your Salvation

1 — Admit You Are A Sinner

For all have sinned, and come short of the glory of God;

~ Romans 3:23 KJV

2 — Realize The Penalty For Sin

For the wages of sin is death; but the gift of God is eternal life through Jesus Christ our Lord.

~ Romans 6:23 KJV

3 — Believe Jesus Died, Was Buried, And Rose Again For You

Christ died for our sins according to the scriptures; And that he was buried, and that he rose again the third day according to the scriptures:

~ 1 Corinthians 15:3-4 KJV

4 — Understand That Salvation Is By Faith Alone

For by grace are ye saved through faith; and that not of yourselves: it is the gift of God: Not of works, lest any man should boast.

~ Ephesians 2:8-9 KJV

5 — Trust Christ Alone As Your Savior, Not Your Behavior

Jesus saith unto him, I am the way, the truth, and the life: no man cometh unto the Father, but by me.

~ John 14:6 KJV
